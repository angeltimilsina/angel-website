Rock Garden Resort Pvt.Ltd

Rock Garden Resort

In Sarangkot’s secluded foothills, Rock Garden oozes Nepali Culture and tradition. Nepalese artifacts decorate the grounds capturing stories of the past within an extraordinary setting of complete tranquility and beauty. Nuzzled within rocky turrets, Rock Garden Resort stands as a fortress above Pokhara Valley as it Lies within the peace of nature. Rock Garden Resort is Separated into different viewpoints within the resort, Rock Garden Restaurant & Bar captures Pokhara valley from a range of perspectives. The luscious garden delicately grows around raised platforms where a variety of Asian and Continental cuisines are served. Rock Garden is the heaven paradise on earth. It is one of the most beautiful places on the lap of nature. It is neither hotel nor resort it is hidden treasure blending with the gigantic sky –crappers of standing rocks.

What is SKYWALK adventure?

Sky Walk adventure activity in Rock Garden Resort, Pokhara is a must try for adventure loving people. Sky walk activity in Rock Garden is 40ft long, walking on the timber wood planks and sequence of ultra-strong wires & cables attached by a harness around the shoulder and waist.
